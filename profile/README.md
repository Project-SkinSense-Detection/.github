SkinSense
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Skin health is a crucial aspect of overall well-being, but accessibility to dermatologists is often hindered by cost and time factors. This condition frequently leads people to ignore their illnesses or even resort to highly risky self-medication. Furthermore, individuals suffering from skin diseases often experience a decrease in self-confidence and strongly need moral support from their surroundings.

To address these challenges, we developed SkinSense, an innovative end-to-end web application solution. SkinSense utilizes a Deep Learning model to classify 15 categories of skin conditions (ranging from Acne to Melanoma) simply through user-uploaded photos. Not only functioning as an early detection tool, this platform is also powered by Generative AI to provide smart condition explanations and initial treatment recommendations. To support mental and emotional recovery, SkinSense also provides an interactive discussion forum that allows users to share stories, treatment experiences, and provide moral support to one another.

Key Features
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* **Skin Disease Image Classification:** Accurately detecting 15 categories of skin conditions using Deep Learning.
* **AI-Powered Explanations & Recommendations:** Providing smart condition explanations and initial treatment guidance via Generative AI.
* **Interactive Discussion & Support Forum:** A safe space for users to share stories, treatment experiences, and provide moral support.

Additional Features
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* **Data Visualization Dashboard:** Interactive insights and analytics regarding skin health data.
* **Responsive Web Design:** A seamless and optimized user experience across both desktop and mobile devices.
